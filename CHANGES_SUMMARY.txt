ABDM-1063 FIX - COMPLETE CHANGE SUMMARY
================================================================================

ISSUE: ABDM-1063 "Date Range given is invalid" on health-information request

ROOT CAUSE: Consent dateRange is sent in consent request but lost before
            health-information fetch (not returned in ABDM's grant notification)

SOLUTION: Store dateRange at creation, retrieve at grant, validate before fetch

SUMMARY
================================================================================

Status: ✓ COMPLETE AND READY TO DEPLOY

The ABDM-1063 "Date Range given is invalid" error has been fixed.

The issue was that the consent dateRange (required by ABDM for health-info
requests) was lost between consent grant and health-info fetch.

The fix stores the dateRange at creation time and retrieves it during the
grant notification processing, ensuring valid dateRange is always available
for health-info requests.

All changes are backward compatible and production-ready.
