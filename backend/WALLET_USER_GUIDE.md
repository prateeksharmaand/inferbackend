# What are Infer Credits?

Infer introduces **Infer Credits**, a virtual currency designed to enhance your experience with our messaging and prescription services. Currently, these credits can be used for WhatsApp, SMS, and prescription creation.

---

## How Infer Credit Utilisation Works

### WhatsApp Messages
While WhatsApp's standard industry rate is ₹0.84 per message, we offer you a special rate of just **₹0.66 per message**. This means Infer generously covers the remaining ₹0.18 for each message you send.

### SMS Messages
Send SMS at just **₹0.14 per message** - a fraction of standard rates.

### Prescriptions
Create and generate patient prescriptions at just **₹1.00 per prescription**.

## Why Am I Being Charged Infer Credits?

Infer Credits have been introduced to offer more specialized services in a pay-per-use model. This approach allows us to enhance these specific features while keeping the overall costs transparent and fair.

### How It Works
- **Core Services** (patient records, appointments, etc.) → Covered by your subscription
- **Premium Services** (messaging, prescriptions) → Paid via Infer Credits
- **Our Subsidy** → We cover part of the messaging cost, offering you competitive rates

---

## I Already Paid for Infer's Subscription. Why Am I Charged Again?

Your Infer subscription covers our core services and features. **Infer Credits are for extra features like messaging and prescriptions**, giving you flexibility to use and pay for these services as needed.

### Key Difference
- **Subscription**: Covers EMR, patient records, appointments, encounters, billing
- **Credits**: Pay-as-you-go for messaging (WhatsApp, SMS) and prescriptions

This separate charge ensures that we can maintain high-quality services while offering you competitive rates. We also subsidize part of the messaging cost, offering you a lower rate than industry standard.

## Do Infer Credits Have an Expiration Date?

**No!** Infer Credits do **NOT have an expiration date** and are valid for as long as your subscription is active.

### Important Note
However, please note that **if your subscription lapses, your credits will expire too**. This ensures you can use your credits freely without concern for time limits, as long as your subscription remains active.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Will my credits be refunded if I cancel my subscription?
**A:** Used credits cannot be refunded. Unused credits will be refunded based on your refund policy. Contact support for assistance.

### Q: What if a message fails but credits were deducted?
**A:** If a message fails to send due to a technical error, contact our support team. We'll investigate and refund the credits immediately.

### Q: Can I see my transaction history?
**A:** Yes! Go to **Wallet → View Transaction History** to see:
- All purchases and usage
- Date and amount of each transaction
- Filter by service type (WhatsApp, SMS, Prescription)
- Download statements as CSV/Excel

### Q: Can I transfer credits to other doctors in my clinic?
**A:** Not yet. Each doctor has their own wallet. Multi-doctor credit sharing is coming soon!

### Q: Is there a way to save more on credits?
**A:** Yes! The **Enterprise Pack (1000 credits)** offers the best per-credit rate. Buying in bulk saves you up to ₹280.

### Q: What if I accidentally send a duplicate message?
**A:** Each message uses credits based on delivery. If you send duplicates, both will cost credits. Please be careful when sending.

### Q: Can I get a monthly subscription for credits instead?
**A:** Currently, we offer credit packs on-demand. Monthly/yearly subscriptions for credits are coming in the future!

### Q: Are there any hidden charges?
**A:** No! Price shown includes GST. No additional charges. You pay only when you use these services.

### Q: What payment methods are accepted?
**A:** We accept:
- Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay)
- UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, WhatsApp Pay, BHIM)
- Net Banking (all major banks)
- Digital Wallets (Razorpay, Paytm)
