Security & Privacy

Your health data stays
private and protected

We apply clinical-grade security practices so you can store sensitive medical records with confidence.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

All health records and personal data are encrypted at rest and in transit using AES-256 and TLS 1.3.

🛡️

JWT Auth & Refresh Tokens

Secure token-based authentication with short-lived access tokens and automatic refresh — no passwords stored in plain text.

🔒

You Own Your Data

Export or delete your complete health record at any time. We never sell or share your data with third parties.
